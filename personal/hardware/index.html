 2026.Jul.13

  • lg ultrafine 32" 4k 144hz monitor - it’s overkill for my lower-midrange pc desktop but it was a good deal. i still feel weird about having it, but no one wants to buy it. oh well.

  • hercules inpulse 300 mk2 - good dj controller. well, the only one i’ve ever had so i don’t have a good perspective on this topic but it works. a bit big though.

  • mx master, keychron k4

  • desktop: ryzen 3 3100, gtx 1660 super, 32gb ddr4 @ 3600, fedora linux. i live out of my mac now so its pretty much just relegated to games and vr.
